About the song:

Don't stop the clocks is a song about the passage of time and the seasons.

It it one of the less popular songs from the album "Sympa", but my personal favourite.

About the band:

King gnu was formed in 2013 and plays the genres of rock, pop and electronic music.

The members are Daiki Tsuneta, Satoru Iguchi, Kazuki Arai and Yu Seki.
